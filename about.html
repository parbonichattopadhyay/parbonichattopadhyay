Background

I'm Parboni Chattopadhyay, a recently graduated Informatics major with a Computer Science minor at UC Irvine. I'm a data-driven software engineer and aspiring product manager passionate about building human-centered applications that translate research and data insights into meaningful impact.

My work spans full-stack development, LLM-assisted data analysis, and clinical research software. I engineered gamified cognitive assessment tools for a pediatric sleep research lab at UCI School of Medicine — work that earned 2nd place nationally at the 2026 Capstone Design Conference (Texas A&M) and 3rd place at the UCI ICS 2026 Senior Design Expo, and was featured in UCI ICS News.

I also conduct research on AI tool adoption among ICS students using LLM-assisted Codex pipelines — research accepted to ICCSE 2026. Previously I interned at Numecent and participated in the NASA L'Space Research Program. I'm interested in roles across software engineering, product management, educational technology, and opportunities that have a passion for innovative design.
